Designing the Solution

A Calm Routine That Starts The Moment You Sit

StillChair orchestrates boredom so your mind can reset. Hardware, software, and environment sync to automate stillness and remove friction from disconnection.

What We Built

An AI-powered, IoT-connected chair, paired with the StillSpace cloud and ambient room controls. One tap authenticates you, the chair senses posture, and the ecosystem shifts into Boring Mode.

Why It Works

A cue-triggered routine replaces digital noise with calm structure. Like a personal trainer for your downtime, StillChair nudges healthy habits and keeps employees accountable without effort.

Problems Solved

  • Break dependency on phones for guidance
  • Reduce decision fatigue around rest
  • Provide incentives and structure for doing "nothing"
  • Create calm rooms that protect focus and privacy

User Journey

Sit -> Session starts -> Devices silence and space dims -> Guided stillness unfolds -> Session ends, insights sync to the app and dashboards.

Smart Chair

Built-In Intelligence, Effortless Reset

StillChair wraps bio-sensing hardware, haptic feedback, and on-chair guidance into one elegant form. Every detail is tuned to reduce friction and keep you unplugged without missing critical signals.

Biosensor Dock

Embedded ECG, respiratory, and EEG sensors read your calm signature while respecting privacy.

Adaptive Haptics

Optional gentle pulses keep you grounded in the moment--no audio, no distractions.

ANC Headset

Noise-cancelling headphones remove ambient chatter and provide tactile cues for transitions.

ID Tap Zone

Contactless authentication for office users enables shared calm rooms with personal insights.

Micro-Recline

Fine-tuned recline mechanism supports stillness without putting you to sleep.

On-Chair Display

Mini display guides your session and reduces phone reliance for controls or coaching.

Phone Slot

Dock your device out of sight; the ecosystem handles status updates and notifications.

StillSpace App

Your Calm Companion, Anywhere

The app keeps your calm score climbing with session history, personalized modes, and live biofeedback. Build custom routines, sync with the chair, and give your team visibility into their recovery habits.

Dashboard

See active sessions, connected devices, and the Calm Score evolving in real time.

Custom Modes

Create or duplicate modes to suit deep work, micro-breaks, or end-of-day decompression.

Bio Stats

Visualize heart rate variability, breathing patterns, and focus levels to understand progress.

Session History

Track trends, export reports, and give teams insight into wellness adoption.
